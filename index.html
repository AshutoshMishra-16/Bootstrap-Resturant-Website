About Us

Being the land of many kings and kingdoms, we have always been fascinated with the lives and lifestyles of the royals. Their opulent homes and larger than life stories created a picture of decadence and lavish living. Times have changed but they yet are a source of interest. One major curiosity factor was their cuisine. What did the king and his family eat?

Till a few decades ago, the only way to enjoy royal cuisine was visually. Some pictures in magazines was all you could savour. Now no more! Curry Kingdom proudly presents some hidden gems from the many royal kitchens across the country. These dishes promise to provide you with a taste of aristocracy as well as a glimpse of a way of life that was. Made with the original recipes and ingredients, each creation is a discovery of hidden flavours and subtle aromas.

But it's not just about the food. We take pride in our warm and inviting atmosphere, where friends and families can come together to enjoy great food and create lasting memories. Whether you're looking for a cozy dinner for two or a place to celebrate a special occasion, Curry Kingdom is the perfect choice.
